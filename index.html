Dark Descent

How to play

WASD or Arrow keys to move

'Run' into enemies to fight them

Find the hole to descend to the next floor!

Tips

Your stats are displayed in the top left corner

Useful notifications appear in the bottom left

The bottom right corner shows the floor and remaining enemies

Dark Descent is a rogue-like dungeon crawler developed over the course of two weeks using vanilla JavaScript in Feb 2017.
